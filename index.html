About Skilvanta

Skilvanta was founded in 2021 with a simple mission: to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements. We noticed that millions of talented students struggle to find meaningful internships, while companies struggle to find skilled candidates.

Our solution? Create a platform where students don't just learn‚Äîthey earn. Through our paid internship programs, students work on real projects, receive mentorship from industry experts, and get compensated for their contributions.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our internship programs.

Once you're selected for our internship program, you'll work on real projects under the guidance of industry mentors. Based on your performance and the quality of work delivered, you earn a stipend ranging from ‚Çπ5,000 to ‚Çπ15,000 per month. Stipends are credited directly to your bank account at the end of each month.

Our selection process has 3 simple steps: 1) Submit your application with basic details and resume. 2) Complete a brief online assessment relevant to your chosen domain (takes about 30-45 minutes). 3) Attend a short interview with our team. Selected candidates receive offer letters within 48 hours of the interview.

Yes! Our certificates are recognized by 50+ hiring partner companies. Each certificate comes with a unique verification ID and QR code that employers can verify on our website. Many of our students have successfully used these certificates to secure jobs at companies like TCS, Infosys, Wipro, and startups.

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with the program within the first 7 days of starting, you can request a full refund. After 7 days, refunds are processed on a prorated basis. Please read our complete Refund Policy for detailed terms.

No prior experience is required for most of our programs. We have beginner-friendly tracks designed for students who are just starting out. Our mentors will guide you from basics to advanced concepts. However, some advanced programs may require basic knowledge of relevant tools or technologies.

The time commitment varies by program. Generally, you'll need to dedicate 15-25 hours per week for part-time programs and 35-40 hours for full-time programs. We offer flexible schedules that can be adjusted around your college timings. You can work on weekends if that suits you better.

Placement assistance is included in Professional and Premium plans. We provide resume reviews, mock interviews, LinkedIn optimization, and referrals to our 50+ hiring partners. While we cannot guarantee a job (as it depends on your skills and interview performance), 85% of our students who complete the program and actively participate in placements get hired within 3 months.

We accept all major payment methods through Razorpay: Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, RuPay), Net Banking (all major banks), UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), and Wallets. EMI options are available on select credit cards with no additional cost.

Yes, you can request a domain switch within the first 7 days of starting the program. After that, switching may be possible subject to availability and a nominal fee. Contact our support team at support@skilvanta.in to discuss your options.

You'll need a laptop or desktop with at least 4GB RAM (8GB recommended for technical programs), a stable internet connection (minimum 5 Mbps), and a webcam for mentorship sessions. Specific software requirements vary by program and will be shared upon enrollment. Most tools we use are free or have student licenses.

LOR is included in Professional and Premium plans. Students in the Starter plan can request an LOR for an additional fee. LORs are provided upon successful completion of the program and are signed by your mentor and our Founder. They are valuable for higher studies and job applications.

Each certificate has a unique ID in the format SKV-CERT-YYYY-XXXXXX. You can verify any certificate by entering this ID on our Certificate Verification page or scanning the QR code on the certificate. The verification page will display the student's name, program completed, and completion date.

We understand that life happens. You can request a program extension of up to 2 weeks at no additional cost. For longer extensions, there may be a nominal fee. In case of medical or personal emergencies, contact our support team for special considerations.
